General School Rules

  • All students must carry their school ID card daily and produce it when requested.
  • Students should arrive at school 10 minutes before the morning assembly.
    Latecomers may not be allowed to enter without a valid reason.
  • Maintain silence in the corridors and while moving between classes.
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited during school hours.
    If found, devices will be confiscated and returned only to parents.
  • Respect school property. Damage to property will result in penalty and compensation.

Academic Rules

  • Complete all homework and assignments on time.
  • Bring required textbooks, notebooks, and stationery daily.
  • No student shall resort to unfair means during examinations.
    Malpractice will lead to suspension and cancellation of examination.
  • Maintain a minimum of 75% attendance to be eligible for examinations.
  • Seek permission from teachers before leaving the classroom during lesson hours.

Code of Conduct

  • Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students at all times.
  • Bullying, harassment, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
    Such behavior will lead to strict disciplinary action.
  • Use polite language and maintain decorum within school premises.
  • Participate actively in school activities and community service programs.
  • Maintain honesty and integrity in all academic and non-academic matters.

Uniform & Appearance

  • Complete school uniform must be worn on all school days.
  • Hair should be neat, clean, and of natural color.
    Boys: Hair above collar; Girls: Long hair tied properly
  • No jewelry except small earrings for girls (one pair).
  • Nails must be clean, trimmed, and free of nail polish.
  • Shoes should be clean and properly polished daily.

Attendance Rules

  • 75% minimum attendance required for promotion to next class.
  • Leave application must be submitted in advance for planned absence.
  • Medical certificate required for absence exceeding 3 days.
  • Half-day leaves are generally not permitted except in emergencies.
  • Attendance during examination days is mandatory.

Disciplinary Procedures

  • All disciplinary matters will be handled by the Disciplinary Committee.
  • Repeated violations may lead to suspension or expulsion.
  • Parents will be informed of any disciplinary action taken.
  • A fair hearing will be provided before any major disciplinary action.
  • Positive behavior reinforcement is encouraged and rewarded.

Facilities Usage

  • Library books must be returned within the specified period.
  • Computer lab equipment must be handled with care.
    Any damage will be charged to the student.
  • Sports equipment must be returned after use.
  • Keep all facilities clean and tidy.
  • Laboratory safety rules must be strictly followed.

Safety & Security

  • Follow all fire safety and evacuation procedures.
  • Unauthorized persons are not allowed inside school premises.
  • Report any safety hazards immediately to school authorities.
  • First aid facilities are available at the medical room.
  • Emergency contact numbers are displayed in all classrooms.
